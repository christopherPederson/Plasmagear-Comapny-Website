Our Past Work

ClearNano Masks™

*We are looking for mask manufacturers to partner with. Please feel free to contact us if you are interested!

Plasmagear, in collaboration with Health Canada, introduces ClearNano™ Face Mask to overcome communication barriers posed by opaque masks. These transparent masks enable visual cues, reducing stress and fostering emotional connections. They cater to speech reading needs, ensuring clear communication for those with hearing aids or vocal disorders. Manufactured via an electrospinning process, ClearNano™ Face Masks offer full breathability while meeting ISO certification standards for level 1 masks. With superior sound transmission and permeability, they provide an inclusive solution for effective communication.

HEPA filters

*We are looking for HEPA manufacturers to partner with. Please feel free to contact us if you are interested!

Plasmagear introduces Highly Efficient Nanofiber HEPA Filters with Low Pressure Drop, revolutionizing HVAC filtration. These filters, utilizing multilayer nanofiber membrane architectures, achieve MERV13 filtration efficiency with over 40% less pressure drop compared to competitors. The reduced pressure drop translates to approximately 6% less HVAC system energy consumption and 2% less electricity consumption for buildings. Moreover, these filters boast antiviral properties, deactivating viral aerosols by up to 99.99%, enhancing user health and safety.

AirClenz™ and AirShade™

In association with AirClenz™

Introducing our innovative public transportation retrofit ventilation system, equipped with a swarm of air purifiers designed to swiftly capture and purify aerosol contaminants. Consisting of two types of purifiers - Airclenz™ and Airshade™ - our system ensures rapid cleansing of air, addressing the concerns of 57% of travelers regarding air cleanliness. Powered by advanced NanoSpun filter technology, our purifiers effectively safeguard public transport riders from viruses and pollution. With adaptable features tailored to individual needs, our ecosystem offers superior capture efficiency compared to central systems, all at a reduced cost.
