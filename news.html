AirClenz and Plasmagear partnership

February 7th 2023

"Air-Clenz™ and PlasmaGear announced today an exclusive partnership to significantly reduce risks of cross infection from airborne disease when traveling on buses and trains in Canada. ClearNano™ approved for sale in Canada

July 6th 2023

ClearNano™, a revolutionary new transparent face mask developed by Plasmagear, has been approved for sale in Canada. ClearNano™ is the world's first fabric transparent mask that is both breathable and effective at filtering out airborne pathogens. ClearNano™ is made using Plasmagear's proprietary nano-fiber technology. This specialty mask is perfect for medical professionals, teachers, and anyone who wants to protect themselves and others while still being able to communicate clearly.
